Welcome to 'Oh, the Places You've Been!', your new travel tracker. To get started, click 'sign up', and then you can go ahead and sign in. If you haven't created your tracker yet, you can click on 'create' under 'Create your Travel Tracker'. Once you add your first country, then boom! your tracker has been created. You can continue to add countries to your tracker from there. Once you're all set, you can sign on out. Next time you log in, feel free to click 'View your Tracker' and your list will be saved from there! You also have the option to create a bucket list - the next countries on your list. You can rank countries, and while you have the option to give them the same rank, I wouldn't do that unless you're planning a crazy trip to many countries, in which case, good for you. Feel free to update or delete any of the countries on either list (not that you would ever delete one... I hope?) Happy travels!
